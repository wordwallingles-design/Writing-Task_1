“What was the most significant or unexpected discovery you made while researching the relevant aspects of your assigned game?"

To be honest, I thought Roblox was just a simple game for kids, like Minecraft. But I discovered something very unexpected: Roblox isn't just one game, it is a platform where people create their own games.

The most interesting thing I learned is that teenagers build these games using a tool called Roblox Studio. They even use a coding language called Lua. I was surprised to find out that some creators even make real money!

Now I see that Roblox is not just for 'wasting time.' It is a great place to learn about programming and digital art. It is much more creative than I first thought.
