Writing Task Assessment

Critical Thinking Question:

“What was the most significant or unexpected discovery you made while researching the relevant aspects of your assigned game?"

Example:
"To be honest, I thought Roblox was just a simple game for kids, like a basic version of Minecraft. But the most unexpected thing I discovered is that it’s actually not a 'game' at all—it’s a massive platform for creating them. The most significant discovery was finding out that almost every game on the platform is made by the users themselves... I realized that Roblox isn't just about 'wasting time' playing; it's a place where you can learn programming, digital art, and business."
